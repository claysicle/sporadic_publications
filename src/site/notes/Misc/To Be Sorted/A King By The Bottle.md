The Drunken King, 
A sly lazy smile,
He does what he pleases,
His kingdom defiled.

The jester advisor,
jokes on the man.
Mocking their lives,
A fate they cannot withstand. 

The Crown,
The Jest.
A bottle,
The rest.

A King on his throne,
A stupor of liquor.
A bottle drank fast,
Another one quicker.

A frown,
A test. 
A bottle,
I guess.

Approached by the mob,
The tyrant’s anger wells.
But none to stand by him, 
All gone but his bells.
  

The Jester has left,
Flee’d from the scene.
And out on the castle floor,
Rolls the guillotine.

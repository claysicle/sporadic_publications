The spirit of modernity 
Died in me tonight.
That is what 
The learned man said.

Spirit toiling eternally
Lost its endless fight -
Yet more life
Remains ahead.

What to do,
With infinity
Over and done?
What to do,
With becoming
Left as one?

I suppose we linger on.
We drift in airless rooms.
I suppose we strive along,
Timeless through these dunes.

In frozen factuality,
In these stasis states.
In patient perpetuity,
Deified, we wait.

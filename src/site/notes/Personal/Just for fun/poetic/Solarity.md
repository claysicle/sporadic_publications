Do you see what I see?
We are stars, you know.
And I see solidarity,
here in our solarity.

Do you see It?
Do you see It?
Do you see It? 
Do you see It?

Have you felt It?
Do you love Her?
Do you know Him?
Are you one of Them?

I know I am.
I know I do.
I feel It.
I know.

Do you?
